## Recap

For anyone not familiar with RPU, a quick recap (we had previously [written about raw.pixls.us](https://pixls.us/blog/2017/01/new-year-new-raw-samples-website/) earlier this year).
There used to be a website for housing a repository of raw files for as many digital cameras as possible called [rawsamples.ch][].
It was created by Jakob Rohrbach and had been running since March of 2007.
Back in 2016 the site was hit with a SQL injection attack that left the [Joomla][] database corrupted (in a teachable moment, the site also didn't have a database backup).

[rawsamples.ch]: http://rawsamples.ch/
[Joomla]: https://www.joomla.org/

With the rawsamples.ch site down, @LebedevRI and @andabata worked to get a replacement option in-place and working: https://raw.pixls.us!

## Sexy Stats

We grabbed all the files we could salvage from rawsamples.ch and @andabata setup the new page.
We've had a slowly growing response as folks have filled in gaps for camera models we still don't have.

## Moar samples!

As @LebedevRI has said, we still really need folks to check [RPU][] and send us more samples!

* We currently only have [about 77% coverage](http://www.darktable.org/resources/camera-support/).
* We want to replace any non-[CC0][] (public domain) samples with [CC0][] licensed samples.
* We are still missing some rarer samples like any medium-format or Sigma samples.

Our hope is that some casual reader out there might look at the list and say "Hey! 
I've got that camera lying around - let me submit a sample!".

If you have any of the cameras on this list and don't mind spending a few minutes uploading a sample file, 
we would be very grateful for the help!

Don't forget that we **are** looking for:

  * Lens mounted on the camera, cap off
  * Image in focus and properly exposed
  * Landscape orientation

and we **are not** looking for:

  * Series of images with different ISO, aperture, shutter, wb, lighting, or different lenses
  * DNG files created with Adobe DNG Converter
  * Photographs of people, for legal reasons.

If you don't see your camera on this list, you're not off the hook yet!
We are also looking for files that are licensed very freely...

[CC0]: https://creativecommons.org/share-your-work/public-domain/cc0/
